Hotsilog is a Filipino dish that contains JUST 3 ingredients. This dish can satisfy you for the day with the simple and fun process of making this dish. Also, you can replace the hotdog with cornbeef, longganisa, or whatever you have.



Ingredients: Rice (4 cups), 6 cloves garlic; minced
One half teaspoon of seasame oil
Salt and pepper 1 tablespoon stems of scallions; chopped
Canola cooking oil/Olive oil
Instructions: Brown the garlic. Add luncheon meat or ham, salt them
Turn to high heat before adding rice, mix for 5 minutes
Add eggs (optional), Seasame oil and scallions, mix till well blended
Remove from the fire and serve with Tapa, Tocino or Longganisa.
Signangag is a pinoy meal that is essentially garlic fried rice with meat on the side, this can be cooked during the weekend or meal prepped the night before you have this breakfast.
